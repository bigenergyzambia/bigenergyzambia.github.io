Market Overview

Zambia's Electrical Infrastructure Market

Zambia's electrical infrastructure is undergoing massive expansion due to mining growth, urban development, and the solar energy transition. The country is a net importer of electrical equipment, with strong demand for transformers, cables, panels, and switchgear.

IndicatorValue
Annual Electrical Equipment Imports$80+ million USD
Transformer Imports (HS 850490)$10-11 million USD (~1,650 tons)
Total National Capacity~3,871 MW
Annual Electricity Deficit~1,000 MW
Major SuppliersChina (60%), UAE, India, South Africa, Turkey
