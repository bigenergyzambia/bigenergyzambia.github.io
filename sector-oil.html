Market Overview

Zambia's Lubricants Market

Zambia is not an oil producer and relies entirely on imports for petroleum products and lubricants. With a growing mining sector, expanding agriculture, and increasing vehicle fleet, demand for motor oils and industrial lubricants continues to rise significantly.

Engine Oils

Premium motor oils for all vehicle types and operating conditions.

Industrial Lubricants

Specialized lubricants for mining and industrial equipment.

  • Hydraulic oils (ISO VG 32, 46, 68, 100)

  • Chain and wire rope lubricants

Agricultural Lubricants

Specialized products for tractors and farm machinery.
