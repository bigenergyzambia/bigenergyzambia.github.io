Market Overview

Zambia's Copper Mining Industry

Zambia is one of the world's top copper producers, with copper accounting for approximately 60% of total exports. The country aims to reach 3 million tons of annual copper production by 2031, creating massive demand for mining equipment, spare parts, and support services.
