About Big Energy

Built To Support Serious Business Connections

Big Energy & Resources Limited is a Zambia-based commercial bridge established in 2026. We support companies, project owners, suppliers and investors by helping them structure business requests and connect with relevant commercial parties.

Our priority sectors include solar energy, agriculture, mining, electrical infrastructure, construction materials, industrial products, textiles, cleaning products and trade brokerage.
