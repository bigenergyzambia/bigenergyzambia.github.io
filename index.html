What We Do

Comprehensive Energy & Resource Solutions

Big Energy & Resources Limited is a diversified Zambian company operating across mining, energy, and trade sectors — delivering end-to-end solutions for Africa's growing industrial and agricultural needs.

⛏️ 01

Mining Services

Mining equipment leasing and brokerage. We connect mining companies with the machinery, resources, and expertise they need to operate efficiently across the region.

Equipment Leasing Brokerage Consulting
⚡ 02

Electric Power & Solar

Power generation, transmission and distribution solutions. Solar energy systems and electrical transformation equipment including switchboards, transformer panels, and fuse boxes.

Solar Energy Transformers Distribution Boards
🌱 03

Agricultural Trade

Wholesale distribution and brokerage of agricultural inputs including fertilizers, liquid fertilizers, and specialized agrochemicals for large-scale farming operations across Africa.

Fertilizers Liquid Fertilizers Wholesale
🛢️ 04

Lubricants & Motor Oil

Supply and wholesale distribution of motor oils and industrial lubricants for mining, transport, and agricultural machinery. Ensuring operational continuity across your fleet.

Motor Oil Industrial Lubricants Wholesale
🔌 05

Electrical Infrastructure

Supply and installation support for electrical infrastructure including insurance panels, distribution boards, switchgear, fuse cups, and complete transformation systems.

Switchgear Insurance Panels Fuse Systems

Trade Brokerage

Non-specialized wholesale trade and brokerage services. Acting as the strategic bridge between suppliers and buyers across energy, mining, and agricultural sectors in Africa.

Brokerage B2B Trade Sourcing
