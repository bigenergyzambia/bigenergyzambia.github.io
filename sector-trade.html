Trade Brokerage

End-to-end brokerage services connecting buyers and sellers across Africa and international markets — from Turkey to Zambia and beyond.

Service Overview

Your Bridge to African Markets

Big Energy & Resources offers comprehensive trade brokerage services, connecting international suppliers (especially Turkish manufacturers) with buyers across Zambia and the Southern African region. We handle the entire process — from sourcing and negotiation to logistics coordination and after-sales support.

Turkish-Zambian Trade Bridge

Turkey's total trade with Sub-Saharan Africa reached $25 billion in 2023, with Zambia being a key growth market. We facilitate this trade by providing local representation, market intelligence, and operational support for Turkish exporters.
