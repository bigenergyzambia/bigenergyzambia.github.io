Market Overview

Zambia's Construction Market

Zambia's construction sector is booming due to rapid urbanization, mining expansion, and infrastructure development. Lusaka and Copperbelt cities are experiencing unprecedented growth in residential, commercial, and industrial construction. Most building materials are imported, creating significant opportunities for Turkish manufacturers.

Plumbing & Sanitary

Complete plumbing systems and sanitary ware.
