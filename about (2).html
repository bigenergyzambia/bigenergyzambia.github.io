Who We Are

Combining Turkey's Production Power
with Zambia's Potential
Under One Professional Roof

Big Energy & Resources Limited is a PACRA-registered sourcing, consultancy and brokerage company headquartered in Lusaka, Zambia. We build a strong trade bridge between Turkey and Zambia, serving the business community of both countries.

We are not just a distributor — we offer a corporate unit in the heart of the capital Lusaka that carries your brand, speaks your language and runs toward your goals.

From mining to agriculture, energy infrastructure to industrial equipment — we connect Turkish manufacturers with Zambian buyers, sourcing the best quality products at the most competitive prices.

"Working only with a distributor in Southern Africa means accepting that your product sits on a shelf. Working with Big Energy & Resources means being present in the market yourself."

Big Energy & Resources · Our Core Philosophy

Our Mission

To connect global manufacturers — primarily from Turkey — with Zambia and Southern Africa; to be a reliable, transparent and effective bridge in sourcing, consultancy and logistics. To help our clients achieve up to 70% operational savings.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted corporate base for Turkish and international investors in Southern Africa. To pioneer projects that position Zambia as the center of regional trade and to be the driver of sustainable growth across the African continent.

Our Values

Transparency and honesty at the core of every transaction. No step is taken without client approval. Accountability maintained through administrative discipline and daily reporting. Long-term partnership — not one-off transactions.
