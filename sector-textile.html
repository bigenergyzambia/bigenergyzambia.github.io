Market Overview

Zambia's Textile Market

Zambia is a net importer of textile products, with total imports of $136 million and exports of $53 million (2023). The market is driven by mining industry demand for workwear, expanding hospitality sector, and mandatory school uniform requirements. Turkish textiles are highly regarded for quality, durability, and competitive pricing.

Safety Equipment

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) for workplace safety compliance.

  • Safety helmets and hard hats (with chin straps)
  • Safety glasses and goggles (anti-fog, UV protection)
  • Hearing protection (earplugs, earmuffs)
  • Face shields and welding helmets
  • Gloves (leather, cut-resistant, chemical-resistant)
  • Respirators and dust masks (N95, P100)
  • Fall protection (harnesses, lanyards)

Safety Footwear
