What We Do

Sourcing & Brokerage Solutions

Big Energy & Resources Limited is a Zambian sourcing, consulting and brokerage company. We don't just sell — we research, compare, and find the best quality products at the most competitive prices for our clients. Our main sourcing hub is Turkey, alongside Europe and Asia. From agriculture to mining, energy to logistics — we cover every sector your business needs.
