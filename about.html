Our Story

Incorporated to Make a Difference

Big Energy & Resources Limited was incorporated in 2024 in Lusaka, Zambia — with a clear mission: to become a leading multi-sector company serving Africa's energy, mining, and agricultural industries.

We bridge the gap between international suppliers and local markets, offering brokerage, wholesale distribution, and specialized services across nine key business sectors. Our team combines deep local knowledge with strong international business connections.

Our unique position as a Turkish-Zambian partnership gives us unmatched access to quality manufacturers in Turkey, while maintaining strong roots in the Zambian market.
