Our Story

Incorporated to Make a Difference

Big Energy & Resources Limited was incorporated on in Lusaka, Zambia — with a clear mission: to become a leading multi-sector company serving Africa's energy, mining, and agricultural industries.

We bridge the gap between international suppliers and local markets, offering brokerage, wholesale distribution, and specialized services across six key business areas. Our team combines deep local knowledge with international business connections.

From fertilizers for Zambia's farms to solar panels for off-grid communities, from mining equipment leasing to electrical infrastructure — we are building the backbone of Africa's industrial future.
