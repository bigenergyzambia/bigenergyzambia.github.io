Who We Are

Two Strengths.
One Reliable Partner.

Big Energy & Resources Limited is a PACRA-registered company headquartered in Lusaka, Zambia. We bridge Turkish and European manufacturers with buyers across Zambia and Southern Africa.

On one hand, we operate in large-scale industrial and government projects — mining, agriculture, energy and electrical infrastructure. On the other, we provide office and staff support for Turkish and European SMEs seeking to enter the African market.

Whether you are a global manufacturer or a small factory seeking your first African client — we are the bridge, the base and the team on the ground.

"We don't just find suppliers. We become your local team — so you can grow in Africa without setting up a company, hiring staff, or leaving home."

Our Mission

To connect global manufacturers — primarily from Turkey — with Zambia and Southern Africa. To be a reliable, transparent bridge in sourcing, brokerage, consultancy and logistics. To help clients achieve up to 70% in operational savings.
